Detailed Description

template<typename T>
class edsp::oscillators::triangular_oscillator< T >

The class triangular_oscillator generates a triangular signal.

The signal increases linearly from -1 to 1 in \( [0, \pi ] \) interval, and decreases linearly from 1 to -1 in the interval \( [ \pi , 2 \pi] \).

The triangle wave shares many geometric similarities with the sawtooth wave, except it has two sloping line segments.
